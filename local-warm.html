The lead — and the difference

No clubs leave the door without a proper fitting.

And it's free with every set. Our in-house master club fitter dials in lie, length, shaft flex, grip size and driver loft — then helps build your set make-up around the way you actually play. Not the way a catalogue says you should.

For every single degree your irons sit off — too flat or too upright — you can miss your target by up to 21 feet.
And that's on a flawless swing. Numbers are tighter than they look.

We're not here to upsell you a name. We're here to put the right clubs in your hands and make sure they fit, so your good swings actually go where you aimed.

  • Irons

    Lie & loft, length, shaft flex, grip size.

  • Driver & Woods

    Loft, shaft flex, length, head settings.

  • Wedges

    Bounce, grind, gapping.

  • Putter

    Length, lie, loft.

Certified clubmaker on hand for re-grips, re-shafts and repairs, too. Bring in the old gamer — we'll bring it back to life.

Our story

A family shop, in the truest sense.

We've been part of Winnipeg golf since 1982 — 40-plus years on Portage Avenue, through every season this province can throw at us. Founded by the Keats family, and still run by them today.

Owner Darrin Keats is on the floor most days, ready to talk through your game over the counter. That's not a marketing line — it's just how a family shop works. Proud to be Manitoba's #1 locally owned and operated golf store.

“This is our community, and your game is our business.”

You'll see our name around town, too — charity scrambles, wedding socials, the small stuff that makes a city feel like home. We're not just selling clubs; we're neighbours who happen to be very good at golf gear.
