The Caddy Shed Difference

No Set Leaves Un-Fit.

It's a promise we've kept since 1982.

Why does custom fitting matter so much? For every single degree your irons sit off — too flat or too upright — you can miss your target by up to 21 feet. And that's on a flawless swing.

So no set walks out our door until it's dialed in to you. Our in-house master club fitter sets your lie, length, shaft flex, grip size and driver loft, then helps you build the set make-up that actually suits your game.

Best part? It comes free with every set we sell — because clubs that fit you, physically and financially, are the whole point.

Our Story

Owned by Manitobans, for Manitobans.

The Caddy Shed has been part of Winnipeg golf since 1982 — more than forty years on Portage Avenue. It's a family shop in the truest sense: founded and still run by the Keats family, with owner Darrin Keats on the floor most days, ready to talk through your game.

We're proud to be Manitoba's #1 locally owned and operated golf store — big enough to carry the best names in the game, small enough to know your name when you walk in.

You'll see us around town, too — from charity scrambles to wedding socials. This is our community, and your game is our business.
