The math of a missed target

One degree off.

For every single degree your irons sit off — too flat or too upright — you can miss your target by up to 21 feet. And that's on a flawless swing. The best swing in the world can't out-hit the wrong specs. That's exactly why we fit before you buy.

Our Story

Owned by Manitobans,
for Manitobans.

For more than 40 years, the Caddy Shed has stood on Portage Avenue as Manitoba's #1 locally owned and operated golf store. Run by the Keats family, it's a place where the inventory is huge but the welcome is personal.

Owner Darrin Keats is on the floor most days — the same person who'll fit your irons, talk through your set make-up, and remember your name next season. That's the difference local ownership makes.
