Pick a Direction

Same shop, three personalities

Every version has the same content — the free-fitting promise, the brands, your reviews, hours and map. What changes is the feel.

Direction 01

Premium Heritage

Refined · Editorial · Timeless

The private-club feel. Elegant serif type on cream paper, deep green, a single brass thread. Quiet and trustworthy — 40 years of reputation, dressed the part.

  • Magazine-style numbered sections
  • Calm whitespace, fine detailing
  • Best for an upscale, classic impression

Direction 02

Modern Sport

Bold · Dark · Performance

The big-brand energy. Dark forest backdrop, an electric green accent, oversized type. The "21 feet per degree" stat becomes a showpiece. Feels brand-new and athletic.

  • Fitting shown like a tech spec sheet
  • Animated stat + sticky "Book a Fitting"
  • Best for standing out as the modern shop

Direction 03

Local & Warm

Friendly · Family · Community

The neighbour's shop that happens to be excellent. Warm cream and honey tones, friendly type, the Keats-family story front and centre. Makes a stranger feel like a regular.

  • Story-forward, people-first
  • Live "Open now / Closed" indicator
  • Best for leaning into local & trusted
