01 — Custom Fitting

A set built to your swing.

Our in-house master club fitter sets your lie and length, shaft flex, grip size and driver loft — then helps build your set make-up around the way you actually play. It is the lead of everything we do, and it is free with every set.

For every single degree your irons sit off — too flat or too upright — you can miss your target by up to 21 feet. And that's on a flawless swing. The case for a proper fitting

04 — Our Story

A golf store owned by Manitobans, for Manitobans.

Since 1982, the Caddy Shed has been a fixture on Portage Avenue — more than forty years of fitting Winnipeg golfers into clubs that actually suit them. It's run by the Keats family, and owner Darrin Keats is on the floor most days.

That's the difference local ownership makes. No commission-chasing, no upsell. Just a fair conversation about your game, an honest fitting, and the gear that'll help you shoot lower scores — backed by a shop that wants you there.

From wedding socials to charity functions, you'll see the Caddy Shed name across the community. We're golfers, neighbours and citizens of this city first.

Darrin KeatsOwner & on the floor, most days
